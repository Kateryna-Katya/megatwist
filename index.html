Forest Dreams

Where the night forest awakens and ancient spirits begin to move.

Forest Dreams basically drops you into this mysterious woodland right after sunset. It's one of those places where even the silence means something, and every little movement you make feels deliberate. Inspired by Japanese mythology and the gentle magic of nature, the game unfolds slowly, revealing spirits, symbols, and hidden energy as you explore deeper into the forest
