Forest Dreams

Where the night forest awakens and ancient spirits begin to move.

Forest Dreams basically drops you into this mysterious woodland right after sunset. It's one of those places where even the silence means something, and every little movement you make feels deliberate. Inspired by Japanese mythology and the gentle magic of nature, the game unfolds slowly, revealing spirits, symbols, and hidden energy as you explore deeper into the forest

Forest mechanics

When combinations include Wild symbols, the forest enters a Night phase. Five glowing yellow spheres appear on the field, marking places of gathered energy.

Wild symbols move toward these spheres, collecting their energy and contributing to the awakening of the Spirit of the Forest. The whole thing has this ritualistic vibe to it - slow, deliberate, and honestly pretty relaxing to watch.

Peekaboo Wild

If a Wild symbol appears during a successful round but is not part of a completed combination, it doesn’t disappear. Instead, it moves into a hidden reserve.
At the right moment — during a calm round or a Night Respin — the hidden Wild quietly returns to the forest, rejoining the flow when it’s least expected.
